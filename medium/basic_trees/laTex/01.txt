Definition\ : \ A\ tree\ is\ a\ finite\ set\ of\ one\ or\ more\ nodes\ such\ that\\
(1)\ There\ is\ a\ specially\ designed\ node\ called\ the\ root\\
(2)\ The\ remaining\ nodes\ are\ partitioned\ into\ n\geq 0\ disjoint\ sets\ T_1,\ ...\ T_n,\ where\ each\ of\ these\ sets\ is\ a\ tree.\ T_1,\ ...\ T_n\ are\ called\ the\ subtrees\ of\ the\ root.
